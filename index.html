OBJECTIVES

As a collective we will:

  • Empower and inspire the next generation
  • Leave a lasting impact
  • Change the world as we know it
  • Be the change we wish to see
  • Revolutionise the current education system
  • Create opportunities for young people to realise their own potential and power
  • Offer a new fresh approach/ perspective to already existing education models
